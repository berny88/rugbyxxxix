The rules of the game

Principles :

  • Entering bets up to 2 hours before the start of matches
  • At the end of each match, results are recorded by the administrator and each participant's points are calculated and ranked.
  • The spirit of the calculation of the points is to leave a "certain" part to the random to leave all its chances to each one, connaisseur or not

Calculation of points

  • If the bettor has found the winner (or guessed a draw): +5 points.
  • +3 points if the bettor has guessed the number of points of a team.
  • +3 points if the bettor guessed the good difference of points between the 2 teams (no matter the winner).
       or +2 points if the bettor has guessed a difference of points (between his bet and the match) between 1 (incl) and 5 points (incl).
       or +1 point if the bettor has guessed a difference of points (between his bet and the match) between 6 (incl) and 10 points (incl).

  • Therefore, for each match, a bettor can collect 5 + (3 + 3) + 3 points = 14 points if he guesses the exact result of the match.
  • Example: TeamA vs TeamB : 12 - 5
    • Bet : 12 - 5 => 14 pts (5 + 3 + 3 + 3) won
    • Bet : 5 - 12 => 3 pts won
    • Bet : 12 - 10 => 10 pts (5 + 3 + 2) won
    • Bet : 12 - 6 => 9 pts (5 + 3 + 1) won
    • Bet : 12 - 0 => 8 pts (5 + 3) won
    • Bet : 10 - 0 => 6 pts (5 + 1) won
    • Bet : 11 - 4 => 8 pts (5 + 3) won

Rankings (for each community)

  • A classification will be made for the group stages
  • A classification will be made for the knockout phases
  • A ranking will be carried out on the whole competition

Appeals

  • No appeal will be accepted (dura lex, sed lex)
